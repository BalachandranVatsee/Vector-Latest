THE VECTOR ADVANTAGE

CHALLENGE 01

THE DICHOTOMY OF THE MARKETING & SALE CYCLE

> An organisation can’t spend too much time & money on purely branding related initiatives, as it will affect its ability to scale efficiently.

> Conversely, focusing on short-term conversions without fuelling the funnel at the top will impact its ability to grow over time.

> Thus, Brand & Performance unintentionally cancel each other out due to lack of communication & integration.

CHALLENGE 02

THE INEFFICIENCIES OF THE CUSTOMER DECISION JOURNEY

> Businesses are serviced by fragmented service solution providers who have little to no oversight over the CDJ funnel.

> The result - brand, communication, digital and media service providers deliver a disjointed brand experience that limits it's potential.

INTRODUCING

AN EFFICIENT CONSUMER-CENTRIC APPROACH

> The mobile-first consumer doesn’t differentiate between Brand and Direct Response Communication.

> Everything is a BRAND EXPERIENCE!

> Everything is about PERFORMANCE

THE VECTOR ADVANTAGE

AN INTEGRATED CONSUMER EXPERIENCE

> An integrated performance and branding approach can smoothen the transition from initiation to deployment and reporting.

> Performance branding can ensure consistency across campaigns and communications.

> An integrated team has the ability to act quickly based on actionable data and business goals; and adopt strategies to maximize ROMI & ROAS.

PURPOSE

For today’s finger scrolling, #Tagtrending, in the moment consumer, we understand that speed and agility is key to maximizing and leveraging brand & business impact opportunities
Brand initiatives are directly measured by ROMI and you cannot improve what you do not measure. We deliver strategic goal based solutions in a scientific, transparent and measurable manner.
In a world of near infinite choices and dwindling differentiation, we believe that creatively distinct brand narratives and interventions build & sustain positive consumer mental availability.
Brand purpose is key for today’s consumers’ who are relentlessly seeking meaning through their purchases. We ensure that the brand credo is reflected undiluted through the funnel.

OUR TEAM

Joseph George

Group Chairman & CEO

After more than 26 uninterrupted years, Joe resigned from Lintas as the Group Chairman & CEO for India, the Regional President for South & Southeast Asia and a Global Management Committee member of the MullenLowe Group network. Double hatting business and strategic planning roles right through, Joe was particularly very closely involved on the Unilever business with local, regional and global remits.

Joe founded Tilt with the vision of contemporarising the way brand and communication agencies are skilled, structured and outputting - for the changing consumer, the changing marketplace and the changing needs of marketers today.

Hari Krishnan

Group Chief Business Officer

With stints across Lowe Lintas, Grey, JWT, Star TV and Mullen Lintas, Hari has over 25 years of experience – six of which were as CEO at MullenLowe Group, Sri Lanka and at Mullen Lintas, India. Hari’s experience straddles Automotive, BFSI, Consumer Durables, ECommerce, Fashion, Fintech, Foodtech, FMCG, Retail, etc. across brands like 3M, Akzo Nobel, Audi, Britannia, Flipkart, Ford, Future Generali, Havells, ITC Foods, PhonePe, Maruti Suzuki, Swiggy, Tanishq, Tata Tea, Titan and Unilever.

Hari is a firm believer that when it comes to brand building, creativity has only one purpose, which is, “mental availability”.

Michelle Suradkar

Group Chief Operating Officer

Over more than 2 decades, Michelle has led organization and culture building and business-led HR practices – starting with the Coca Cola Company, and then in the Mullen Lowe Lintas Group, where she headed the HR function for over 10 years as the Group Chief Human Resources Officer and a member of the Managing Committee.

Michelle has an MBA from Bajaj and is a certified POSH trainer with a particular focus on DEI, and is also a certified Extended DISC administrator.

Gulshan Singh

Group Chief Strategy Officer

Gulshan brings a wide breadth of experience, having spent over 2 decades across research, consulting, and strategic planning, covering India, the Middle East and Europe, building brands for clients including Britannia, GSK, GM, Licious, Livspace, M&M, MakeMyTrip, Marico, Mondelez, Myntra, Nestle, Nivea, P&G, Swiggy, Tanishq and Tata Tea.

Gulshan has an MBA from HEC Paris, specializing in Strategy and Growth.

Adarsh Atal

Group Chief Creative Officer

After almost a decade at Lintas, Adarsh joined Tilt pretty much from its very beginning. He is today amongst India’s youngest ever to lead the creative function in an agency. Not surprising, since he has to his credit some of the best work ever done on Tanishq, Paper Boat, Flipkart, Britannia, Flying Machine, Fastrack, Dream11, Swiggy, Licious, Ultratech, Myntra, Livspace and many others.

An Arsenal fan for 25 years now, a ‘5th generation Marwadi from Nepal’ and ‘Kroaknights Season 3 semi-finalist’ are some of his lesser-known facts.

OUR PARTNERS

TeraReach is India's leading agency for content & influencer marketing which delivers innovative campaigns with industry-leading results for the world’s most prominent brands.
SWIRL is the premier live shopping mobile SaaS to engage, convert and retain customers. It uses video as a backbone to power real-time virtual shopping – helping brands build authentic customer engagements by bridging the online-offline gap.
Entravision MediaDonuts is an online advertising and technology company that helps advertisers achieve their performance and branding goals across digital media channels. MediaDonuts has offices in 11 countries, across EMEA, APAC & US with headquarters in Belgium and Singapore.
